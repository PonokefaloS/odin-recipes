Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry

Description

This Vegan Sweet Potato Chickpea Curry is a comforting and flavorful dish that combines tender sweet potatoes, protein-rich chickpeas, and aromatic spices in a rich, creamy curry sauce. The natural sweetness of the potatoes balances perfectly with the savory and mildly spiced flavors, creating a hearty meal that is both nourishing and satisfying.

Easy to prepare and packed with plant-based ingredients, this curry is ideal for weeknight dinners or meal prep. Served on its own or alongside rice, quinoa, or naan, it offers a delicious combination of textures and flavors that make it a favorite for vegans and non-vegans alike.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 240 g chickpeas, drained
  • 410 g diced tomatoes
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 250–300 g sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 tablespoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoons ground turmeric
  • ½ teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoons red chili flakes
  • 30 g baby spinach

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Cook onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until softened, about 5 minutes. Add chickpeas, tomatoes, coconut milk, and sweet potato. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
  2. Season with garam masala, cumin, turmeric, salt, and chile flakes. Add spinach right before serving.
